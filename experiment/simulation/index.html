Instructions for Performing the Stroop Task
  • When the experiment starts, a word will appear on the screen in a specific color.
  • Your task is to identify the color of the word as quickly as possible, not the meaning of the word.
  • To do this, press the corresponding key on your keyboard:
    • 'g' for green
    • 'b' for blue
    • 'r' for red
    • 'y' for yellow
    • Note: The keys are case sensitive and must be lower case.
  • If the word "RED" is shown in red color, press 'r'. If the word "RED" is shown in blue color, press 'b', and so on.
  • Press the Spacebar to start the experiment when you're ready.
  • Once the experiment is completed, you can click on "Results" to view your response times (in milliseconds) for each trial.
  • Good luck, and enjoy the experiment!

Note: Please wait after clicking space. It may take time to start the experiment.

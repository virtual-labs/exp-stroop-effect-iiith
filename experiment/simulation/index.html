Instructions for Performing the Stroop Task
  • When the experiment starts, a word will appear on the screen in a specific color.
  • Your task is to identify the color of the word as quickly as possible, not the meaning of the word.
  • Press the corresponding key on your keyboard:
    • 'g' for green
    • 'b' for blue
    • 'r' for red
    • 'y' for yellow
  • The keys are case-sensitive and must be lowercase.
  • Press Spacebar to start the experiment.
  • After all trials, click Result to view your response times.

Note: Press Space to begin. Do not refresh until complete.
